Pay Overview

Walmart pays associates an average of $15.50 per hour, with wages ranging from $12.00 to $24.00 per hour depending on position, experience, and location.

All Walmart employees are paid on a weekly schedule. Payment methods include direct deposit and the company pay card program, which provides earlier access to earned wages.

Factors Affecting Pay

Several factors influence how much you can earn at Walmart:

  • Position: Specialized roles like pharmacy technician pay more than entry-level positions
  • Location: Higher cost-of-living areas typically offer higher wages
  • Experience: Long-term associates receive incremental raises
  • Shift: Overnight and weekend shifts may include differential pay

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average pay at Walmart?

The average hourly pay at Walmart is $15.50 per hour. Wages range from $12.00/hr for entry-level positions to $24.00/hr for specialized roles.

How often does Walmart pay employees?

Walmart pays employees on a weekly basis. Pay is typically deposited via direct deposit or available on a pay card.

Does Walmart offer raises?

Yes, Walmart offers annual performance-based raises and periodic minimum wage adjustments. Associates may also receive raises through promotions and role changes.

What is the highest paying job at Walmart?

Among common positions, Pharmacy Technician tends to be the highest paying at approximately $18.50/hr. Management positions typically earn higher salaries.

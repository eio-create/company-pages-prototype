Walmart Background Check: What to Expect in 2026

Background Check Overview

Walmart conducts background checks on all prospective employees as part of the hiring process. This is a standard practice for retail employers and helps ensure a safe workplace.

The background check is typically initiated after a conditional job offer has been made and requires your consent.

What the Background Check Includes

Check TypeDescriptionTimeline
Criminal HistorySearch of criminal records1-3 days
Employment VerificationConfirm past employers2-5 days
Identity VerificationVerify SSN and identity1-2 days
Education (some roles)Verify degrees/certifications2-5 days

Timeline

Most Walmart background checks are completed within 3-5 business days. Factors that may extend this timeline include:

  • Living in multiple states previously
  • International employment history
  • Court record delays
  • Volume of applicants during hiring events

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Walmart do background checks?

Yes, Walmart conducts background checks on all new hires as part of the standard hiring process.

What does the Walmart background check include?

The Walmart background check typically includes criminal history, employment verification, and identity verification.

How long does the Walmart background check take?

Walmart background checks typically take 3-5 business days to complete, though some may take longer.

Can I work at Walmart with a felony?

Walmart considers each applicant individually. Having a criminal record does not automatically disqualify you, but certain offenses may affect eligibility for specific positions.

Retail Store Background Check

Positions: Cashier, Stocker, Cart Attendant, Sales Associate

  • Standard 7-year criminal history search
  • SSN verification and identity check
  • Sex offender registry check
  • Typically completes in 3-5 business days
  • You'll receive a conditional offer before the check begins

Warehouse Background Check

Positions: Warehouse Worker, Order Filler, Forklift Operator

  • Same as retail but considered safety-sensitive
  • Drug test typically runs concurrently
  • You may start training while the background check is processing
  • Forklift certification roles may have additional checks

Pharmacy Technician Background Check

More thorough due to controlled substance access

  • Extended criminal history search (7-10 years)
  • State pharmacy board verification
  • PTCB or ExCPT certification verification
  • DEA database check for controlled substance violations
  • Drug-related offenses are typically disqualifying

Corporate Background Check

Positions: Accountant, Analyst, Manager, IT, HR

  • Comprehensive criminal history (all jurisdictions)
  • Full employment verification (dates, titles, reason for leaving)
  • Education verification (degree, graduation date)
  • Professional license verification (CPA, etc.)
  • Credit check for finance-related roles only
  • Reference checks may be included

Criminal History Guidelines

Walmart follows "ban the box" practices and evaluates criminal history on a case-by-case basis considering:

  • Nature and severity of the offense
  • Time since the offense occurred
  • Relevance to the job duties
  • Evidence of rehabilitation

Note: Certain offenses may disqualify you from specific roles regardless of time passed: theft (cash handling), violence (customer-facing), drug offenses (pharmacy), DUI (driving positions).

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Walmart do background checks for all positions?

Yes, Walmart conducts background checks on all new hires. However, the depth of the check varies: basic checks for hourly retail, more thorough for warehouse (safety-sensitive), and comprehensive for corporate roles.

Can I work at Walmart with a felony?

Walmart evaluates each applicant individually using "ban the box" principles. Having a record doesn't automatically disqualify you, but certain offenses may affect eligibility for specific positions (pharmacy, cash handling, driving).

How long does the Walmart background check take?

Retail: 3-5 days. Warehouse: 3-5 days (but you may start training while it processes). Corporate: 5-10 days with more verification steps.

What if my background check is taking longer than expected?

Delays are normal for applicants with common names, multiple addresses, or out-of-state history. Contact HR if it exceeds 10 business days. Don't assume the worst - delays are usually administrative.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the employee rating for Walmart?

Walmart has an overall employee rating of 3.4 out of 5 stars based on 128,453 reviews from current and former employees.

What do employees like about working at Walmart?

Common positives mentioned in Walmart reviews include flexible scheduling, employee discounts, and opportunities for advancement. Many appreciate the weekly pay schedule.

What are the downsides of working at Walmart?

Employee reviews mention challenges such as fast-paced work environment, varying management quality by location, and physical demands of certain positions.

Would employees recommend Walmart as an employer?

Based on reviews, approximately 60% of employees would recommend Walmart to a friend seeking employment, citing it as a good option for those seeking flexible schedules and entry-level opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the employee rating for Walmart?

Walmart has an overall employee rating of 3.4 out of 5 stars. Ratings vary by position: Retail store 3.1/5, Warehouse 3.3/5, Corporate 3.8/5. Based on 128,453 reviews from current and former employees.

Do warehouse workers rate Walmart higher than retail workers?

Yes, warehouse workers tend to rate Walmart slightly higher (3.3/5 vs 3.1/5) due to consistent schedules, higher pay, and better overtime opportunities. However, they note more physical demands.

What do corporate employees say about working at Walmart?

Corporate employees rate Walmart highest (3.8/5), citing competitive benefits, work-life balance, and career growth. Common concerns include bureaucracy and Bentonville location requirements.

What do retail store employees like about Walmart?

Retail workers appreciate flexible scheduling (can set availability), employee discount (10%), advancement opportunities from within, and weekly pay. Common concerns: fast pace, management varies by store.

Would employees recommend Walmart as an employer?

Recommendation rates vary by position: Retail 55%, Warehouse 62%, Corporate 75%. Overall, about 60% would recommend Walmart to a friend, especially for entry-level opportunities and career growth.

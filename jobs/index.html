About Walmart Employment

Walmart is actively hiring across the United States with 52,847 open positions currently available. The company offers opportunities for job seekers at all experience levels, from first-time workers to experienced professionals.

Most entry-level positions at Walmart require no prior experience, making it an accessible option for those entering the workforce. The company provides on-the-job training for new associates and offers pathways for career advancement within the organization.

Types of Jobs at Walmart

Walmart offers a diverse range of positions including:

  • Store Associates: Cashiers, stockers, customer service, and department specialists
  • Warehouse/Distribution: Order fillers, forklift operators, and logistics coordinators
  • Specialized Roles: Pharmacy technicians, opticians, and automotive technicians
  • Management: Team leads, department managers, and store managers

Frequently Asked Questions

How many jobs does Walmart have?

Walmart currently has 52,847 open positions across the United States. Job openings include roles in retail, warehouse, pharmacy, and management.

What positions are available at Walmart?

Common positions at Walmart include Warehouse Worker, Cashier, Stocker, and more. Positions range from entry-level to management roles.

How do I apply for a job at Walmart?

You can apply for Walmart jobs online through the company careers portal. The application process typically takes 15-20 minutes and includes an assessment.

What is the minimum age to work at Walmart?

The minimum age to work at Walmart is 16 years old for most positions. Some roles, such as operating equipment, require employees to be at least 18.

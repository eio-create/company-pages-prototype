Walmart Employee Benefits: Complete 2026 Guide

Benefits Overview

Walmart offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support associates' health, financial security, and career development. Benefits vary based on employment status (full-time vs. part-time) and tenure with the company.

Full-time associates working 30+ hours per week are eligible for the complete benefits package, while part-time workers have access to select benefits including the employee discount and retirement plans.

Health Benefits

Medical Insurance

Multiple plan options including HMO and high-deductible plans. Coverage for associates and dependents with varying premium levels.

Dental & Vision

Dental coverage for preventive and major services. Vision plan covering exams, glasses, and contacts.

Life Insurance

Company-paid basic life insurance with options to purchase additional coverage.

Disability

Short-term and long-term disability coverage to protect income during extended illness or injury.

Financial Benefits

401(k) Retirement

Walmart matches 6% of associate contributions to the 401(k) plan. Immediate vesting on company match.

Associate Stock Purchase

Purchase company stock at a 15% discount through payroll deductions.

Weekly Pay

Associates are paid on a weekly basis with direct deposit available.

Bonus Programs

Quarterly bonuses based on store performance and attendance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What benefits does Walmart offer?

Walmart offers comprehensive benefits including medical, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) retirement plans with company match, paid time off, employee discounts, and educational assistance programs.

How long until Walmart benefits start?

Full-time Walmart associates typically become eligible for benefits after 90 days of employment. Some benefits, like the employee discount, are available immediately.

Does Walmart offer part-time benefits?

Part-time associates at Walmart have access to limited benefits including the employee discount, 401(k) participation, and the associate stock purchase plan. Health insurance is primarily available to full-time workers.

Does Walmart offer tuition assistance?

Yes, Walmart offers the Live Better U program which provides free college education, high school completion, and skills training for associates. This benefit covers tuition and fees at partner universities.

Hourly Retail Benefits

Positions: Cashier, Stocker, Cart Attendant, Sales Associate, Customer Service

Store-level employees receive the standard hourly benefits package with flexible scheduling advantages.

Flexible Scheduling

Set your availability for mornings, evenings, or weekends. Great for students and second jobs.

Quarterly Bonus (MyShare)

Earn up to $700/year based on store performance and your attendance record.

Immediate Discount

10% off starts day 1. Plus extra 10% on fresh produce.

Promotion Path

Associate → Team Lead → Coach → Store Manager ($100k+ salary).

Hourly Warehouse Benefits

Positions: Warehouse Worker, Order Filler, Forklift Operator, Loader/Unloader

Distribution center employees receive the same core benefits as retail, plus warehouse-specific perks.

Shift Differentials

Extra $1.00-$2.50/hour for overnight shifts (10pm-6am). Weekend differentials at select locations.

Safety Bonuses

Earn up to $100/month for maintaining accident-free work periods.

Consistent Hours

More predictable schedules than retail. Many positions offer guaranteed 40 hours/week.

Climate-Controlled

Most DCs are climate-controlled with break rooms and on-site amenities.

Corporate / Salaried Benefits

Positions: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Software Engineer, HR, Marketing, Manager

Corporate employees at headquarters and regional offices receive an enhanced package.

Day 1 Benefits

Health, dental, vision start immediately. No 90-day wait.

Annual Bonus

Performance-based: 10-20% of salary. Directors+: 20-40%.

Stock Options (RSUs)

Restricted stock units granted annually. 3-year vesting.

15-20 Days PTO

Significantly more time off than hourly roles. Increases with tenure.

Hybrid Work

Many roles offer 2-3 days remote per week. Some fully remote.

Relocation Package

Full relocation assistance for moves to Bentonville HQ.

Benefits for ALL Employees

These benefits are available to every Walmart employee regardless of position type:

10% Employee Discount

Available day 1. Extra 10% on fresh produce.

Free College (Live Better U)

100% tuition paid at partner universities.

401(k) with 6% Match

Immediate vesting. Enroll day 1.

Stock Purchase (15% off)

Buy company stock through payroll.

Associate Assistance

Free counseling and financial coaching.

Parental Leave

Paid leave for birth/adoption.

Frequently Asked Questions

What benefits does Walmart offer?

Walmart offers benefits including medical, dental, vision, 401(k) with match, PTO, employee discounts, and free college. Benefits vary by position type: hourly retail, hourly warehouse, and corporate/salaried employees receive different packages.

Do warehouse workers get different benefits than cashiers?

Hourly warehouse and retail workers share the same core benefits (health insurance, 401k, discount). Warehouse workers may receive shift differentials ($1-2/hr extra for nights) and safety bonuses not available in retail stores.

What benefits do corporate employees get at Walmart?

Corporate/salaried employees receive enhanced benefits: health insurance from day 1 (vs 90 days for hourly), more PTO (15-20 days vs 6 days), annual bonuses (10-20% of salary), stock options (RSUs), and hybrid work options.

How long until Walmart benefits start?

For hourly employees, health benefits start after 90 days. For salaried employees, benefits start day 1. Some benefits like employee discount and 401(k) enrollment are available immediately for everyone.

Does Walmart offer part-time benefits?

Part-time workers (<30 hrs/week) get limited benefits: employee discount, 401(k) with match, stock purchase plan, and free college. Health insurance requires full-time status (30+ hrs/week).

Does Walmart offer tuition assistance?

Yes, the Live Better U program provides 100% free college tuition at partner universities for ALL employees (hourly and salaried). This includes degrees, certificates, and high school completion.

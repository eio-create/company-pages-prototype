Retail Store (Cashier, Stocker, Cart Attendant)

What to Wear

  • Company-provided vest (worn during shift)
  • Solid-colored shirt: navy, white, or black
  • Khaki, black, or dark blue pants/jeans
  • Closed-toe, non-slip shoes

What to Avoid

  • Shirts with logos/graphics
  • Ripped or distressed jeans
  • Open-toe shoes or sandals
  • Shorts (most stores)

Warehouse / Distribution Center

Safety is the priority. Dress code is driven by workplace safety requirements.

Required

  • Steel-toe boots (mandatory)
  • Company t-shirt or hi-vis vest
  • Full-length pants (no shorts)
  • Long hair secured/tied back
  • No loose jewelry

Safety Gear (Provided)

  • Hi-visibility vest
  • Safety glasses (some areas)
  • Hearing protection (some areas)
  • Cut-resistant gloves (as needed)

Note: Some DCs reimburse for steel-toe boots after 90 days. Check with HR about the boot voucher program.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy dress code is more conservative for professional appearance.

  • Scrubs (solid colors) OR business casual with pharmacy coat
  • Company-provided pharmacy coat must be worn
  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • Conservative hair colors preferred
  • Minimal jewelry (hygiene/safety)
  • Visible tattoos may need to be covered

Corporate / Office

Business casual is standard, though it varies by department and meeting schedule.

Day-to-Day

Business casual: collared shirts, blouses, dress pants, clean jeans on Fridays (some offices).

Client/Exec Meetings

Business professional: suits, blazers, dress shoes. Plan ahead for important meetings.

Personal Appearance (All Positions)

Generally Allowed

  • Colored/dyed hair (retail, warehouse)
  • Visible tattoos (non-offensive)
  • Ear piercings (multiple OK)
  • Neat facial hair/beards

May Have Restrictions

  • Facial piercings (pharmacy, some roles)
  • Offensive/inappropriate tattoos
  • Strong fragrances (all positions)
  • Extreme styles (depends on role)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code for Walmart cashiers?

Cashiers wear company vest over solid-colored shirt (navy, white, or black) with khaki or black pants. Closed-toe non-slip shoes required.

What do Walmart warehouse workers wear?

Warehouse workers wear safety-compliant attire: company t-shirt or hi-vis vest, sturdy pants (no shorts), steel-toe boots required. Hair must be secured.

What is the dress code for pharmacy at Walmart?

Pharmacy technicians wear scrubs or business casual with company-provided pharmacy coat. Closed-toe shoes. More conservative appearance standards.

Can you have tattoos at Walmart?

Retail: Visible tattoos generally allowed (non-offensive). Warehouse: Tattoos OK but safety gear takes priority. Pharmacy: Visible tattoos may need to be covered. Corporate: Business judgment applies.

Retail Store Positions (Cashier, Stocker, Cart Attendant)

Pre-Employment Testing

Walmart has reduced mandatory drug testing for most store-level positions. Policy varies by state and location. Many markets no longer require pre-employment testing for these roles.

When You May Be Tested

  • After a workplace accident/injury
  • For cause (suspected impairment)
  • Before promotion to safety-sensitive role

Warehouse & Distribution Center

Pre-employment drug testing is REQUIRED for all warehouse positions due to safety-sensitive nature of the work.

Test Type

Urine test (5-panel or 10-panel depending on location)

Timing

After conditional offer, before start date. Must complete within 24-48 hours of notice.

Forklift Operators

Subject to random testing throughout employment. Refusal = termination.

Results Timeline

Typically 24-72 hours. Negative = clear to start. MRO review for positive results.

Pharmacy Technician

Pre-employment drug testing is REQUIRED. Pharmacy staff have access to controlled substances and face the strictest testing requirements.

  • Pre-employment test required (no exceptions)
  • Random testing throughout employment
  • Immediate testing after any medication discrepancy
  • Stricter substances panel may apply
  • Positive test = immediate termination and state board notification

What Substances Are Tested?

Standard panel typically includes:

Marijuana (THC)

Cocaine

Opiates

Amphetamines

PCP

Note on Marijuana: Walmart follows federal workplace safety guidelines. THC is typically included in testing panels even in states where recreational use is legal. Policies are evolving - check current requirements for your location.

Testing Process

1

Receive Instructions

After conditional offer, you'll get a form with testing location and deadline (usually 24-48 hours).

2

Visit Collection Site

Bring valid photo ID. The collector will verify your identity and explain the process.

3

Provide Sample

Urine sample collected under controlled conditions. Temperature verified for validity.

4

Results

Negative results in 24-72 hours. Positive results reviewed by Medical Review Officer (MRO) who may contact you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Walmart drug test cashiers?

Drug testing for retail store associates (cashiers, stockers) varies by location. Many stores have stopped routine pre-employment testing for these roles, but you may still be tested post-accident or for cause.

Does Walmart drug test warehouse workers?

Yes, warehouse and distribution center positions require pre-employment drug testing. This is mandatory for safety-sensitive roles involving equipment operation.

Does Walmart test for marijuana?

Walmart follows federal guidelines. Marijuana is typically included in the standard panel even in states where it's legal. Policies may vary by state and are evolving.

What happens if I fail a Walmart drug test?

A positive result typically disqualifies you from the current application. You may be able to reapply after a waiting period (usually 6-12 months). Some positions offer retesting options.

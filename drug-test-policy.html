Walmart Drug Test Policy: What to Know in 2026

Drug Testing Overview

Walmart's drug testing policy varies by position and location. While not all positions require drug testing, certain roles have mandatory pre-employment screening.

Positions that typically require drug testing include: distribution center workers, pharmacy staff, auto care technicians, and any role involving heavy machinery or driving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Walmart drug test?

Walmart drug testing policies vary by position. Roles involving heavy machinery, driving, or pharmacy typically require pre-employment drug testing.

What type of drug test does Walmart use?

Walmart typically uses urine tests for pre-employment drug screening. Some positions may require additional testing methods.

When does Walmart drug test?

Drug testing at Walmart typically occurs after a conditional job offer, before the start date. Random testing may apply to certain positions.

What does Walmart test for?

Walmart drug tests typically screen for common substances including marijuana, cocaine, opiates, amphetamines, and PCP.

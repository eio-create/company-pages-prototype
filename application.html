Walmart Application: How to Apply in 2026

Application Overview

Applying to Walmart is done online through the company careers portal. The process includes creating an account, completing an application, and taking an assessment test.

Plan for approximately 45 minutes to complete the entire process. Have your work history and references ready before starting.

Application Steps

1

Create Account

Register with your email address on the careers website.

2

Search Jobs

Browse by location, position, or job type. Select positions that match your skills.

3

Complete Application

Enter personal information, work history, and availability. Takes 15-20 minutes.

4

Take Assessment

Complete the required assessment covering work scenarios and personality. Takes 20-30 minutes.

5

Submit & Wait

Submit your application and wait for contact from the hiring team. Check email regularly.

Application Tips

  • Complete the application in one session if possible
  • Be honest about your availability - flexibility helps
  • Double-check contact information for accuracy
  • Take the assessment seriously - answers matter
  • Apply to multiple positions to increase chances

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a job at Walmart?

Apply online through the Walmart careers website. Create an account, search for positions, and complete the application and assessment.

How long does the Walmart application take?

The Walmart application takes approximately 15-20 minutes, plus an additional 20-30 minutes for the required assessment.

Can I apply to Walmart in person?

While Walmart primarily uses online applications, some locations have kiosks. However, online application is recommended for faster processing.

How do I check my Walmart application status?

Log into your Walmart careers account to check application status. You may also receive email updates about your application.
